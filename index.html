Welcome to Earth Science 101

Welcome to Earth Science 101. This course explores the processes that shape our planet, from plate tectonics and rock formation to the atmosphere, oceans, and climate. Our goal is to build a strong foundation in Earth science concepts while encouraging you to think critically about the dynamic systems that affect life on Earth.
